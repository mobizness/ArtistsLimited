Artists Unlimited

Power Your Music


At Artists Unlimited our mission is simple: Connect music listeners with music curators and their audiences. By submitting your music to us, we will ensure that every major music curator, label & blog partnered with Artists Unlimited will get a chance to review your song. There is no submission fee, simply submit and get a chance to be heard.

After giving all our vendors 48 hours to listen to your track, we will send you an email notifying if & where you have been accepted for a repost. From there you are simply a few clicks of a button away from getting your music promoted, and you will be able to track all your reposts in real time. Submit your music and find out who out there believes in your art!
