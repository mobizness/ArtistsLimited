PR Campaigns


Artists Unlimited works closely with artists to engineer PR campaigns around their upcoming releases. We work across all major music streaming services including Soundcloud, YouTube, Spotify, and iTunes, as well as reach out to a variety of blogs, both those established on Hype Machine and otherwise. We have three available plans, though we remain flexible and open to tailoring our campaigns to the individual needs of the artist.
