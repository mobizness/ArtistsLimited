FAQs

How can we help you?

At the core, AU is a team of computer scientists who love music. Simple observations about the online music industry lead to a startling realization: most musical artists are unprepared to use the internet as a tool to advance their careers. By browsing SoundCloud, Spotify, or YouTube and it becomes shocking as to how much talent is left unheard. With AU, we are taking the opportunity to step away from the current paradigm by which radio and mainstream music are monopolized by a handful of labels; we move towards a new vision by which artists truly have a fighting chance to be successful independently.

We are dedicated to making sure every submission that comes our way is heard. We work with multiple clients to ensure organic engagement on various platforms including SoundCloud, Spotify, Facebook, Twitter, and Youtube.

WWe are committed to following every technological twist and turn of how music is promoted and spread on the internet, a task that requires constant awareness of the shifting dynamics of technologies underlying music-sharing platforms. With sensitivity to detail, we have distilled insights and used them to develop user-friendly plugins, tools, and other applications.

We are here to remove the element of chance from becoming known. Working with us, it is no longer a matter of clinging to some hope that you will “blow up” and become viral, or even find substantive but intermittent success. Rather, with us, it is a function of pure promotion across our already well-established networks and gaining exposure to thousands of people who will stream your music through thousands of headphones across the world. We use tried-and-true tools that are essential to producing the highest-quality online presence possible.

What is our vision?

We believe in a world where artists are able to focus on making music while optimizing exposure to audiences everywhere. We are here to democratize the music industry. We believe in a world where a bedroom producer can have the same shot of topping the charts as a producer signed to a major label.

How does a repost submission work?

If you would like to see your track reposted on one of AU’s network brands, simply navigate to the Repost Submission page and submit your track. We will be in touch by email as to whether or not the track has been approved.

Can I schedule the promotion date for my track?

Unfortunately, we currently do not offer this. However, we intend on adding it very soon.

What is a premiere or an exclusive release?

Every week, we choose three tracks for which we fully manage online PR. After submitting your unreleased track via our Premiere Submission page, we will notify you of what we believe is the best course of action for your track. We will:

-Find an appropriate SoundCloud Channel to release your track on SoundCloud
-Promote the track on SoundCloud
-Handle administrative tasks to upload the track via Spotify and all major outlets (if the artist is amendable)
-Create artwork for the track and receive counsel on the best course of action for engaging fans on all social media outlets
-Review an artists online footprint and offer tips and suggestions for improving an artists exposure on the internet

This is free of charge so please submit your unreleased music!

If I am a SoundCloud Channel and I am interested in joining the AU Network, how can I join?

If you would like to join the AU Network, please submit an application in the Apply page. We will be in touch.

As a music curator, what are the benefits of joining the AU Network?

We are not only dedicated to empowering artists directly, but also indirectly. In helping music curators be more effective at releasing music themselves, artists will indirectly benefit. By joining the AU Network, a content curator will have all of the tools and resources to successfully launch each of their musical brands. These are tools and resources we have created by working closely with various online music distributors.
