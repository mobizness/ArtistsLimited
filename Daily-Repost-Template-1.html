Hello, Bonjour, Holá, Ni Hao, Olá und Hallo

 

This is the team behind Etiquette Noir, Le Sol, Classy Records, and many other successful music curators and artists promotion hubs on the internet. We are Artists Unlimited and our mission is to E N A B L E artists, and we are reaching out because we would love to have you submit your music to us for a repost or for an exclusive premiere!

We are trying to be dependable and do our best to please as many producers and artists as we possibly can. With that being said, we have just finished creating two incredibly easy and incredibly effective ways in which we help artists get heard. Our first is through our repost submission system and our second through our premiere submission system (via unreleased@peninsulamgmt.com) 

//

For Repost Submissions, With the help of our tech team, we have completely automated the entire process. When you submit a track, your upload will be submitted to our ENTIRE network, consisting of Artists Unlimited approved Labels, Promotional Network, and Tastemaker Brands. Every contact in this network will have our guarantee of good vibes. These contacts range from team members and friends to people we have met, spoken to, and approved for repost/premiere opportunity. The people who have made these different brands C U R A T E music on these channels and are extremely passionate about trying to please an audience of listeners interested in a specific vibe. We will do our best to connect your music with a channel representing your vibes. 

 

All tracks will be reviewed by the founders of every one of these enterprises. You will then be assigned to a label, promotional network, or tastemaker brand which fits your track. Once you choose which one best fits you, we will only ask for a small fee. This fee is to allow us to continue sustaining our programmers and music curators so we can continue building more and more tools to help you guys out! Once the fee has been completed, the rest will take care of itself. Minutes after, you will receive a email letting you know the exact day your submission will be reposted. You will receive another email the moment the repost occurs. This will facilitate all communication regarding the status of your repost and your submission and, we hope, make it seamless for you. 
 

The process starts by submitting your song to us. Upon submission, you will hear back from us shortly letting you know how we can help. If you are curious, start by submitting your song to us here.
 

For Premiere Submissions, you will need to submit a private link of your UNRELEASED music to unreleased@peninsulamgmt. The tracks approved for premiere will personally be taken are of by our PR team free of charge. We are currently designing a PR system which will be done over the next couple of months, allowing the fantastic work of our PR team to extend passed our releases; we will tell you more about this at the end of the email! The team typically guarantees around 100,000 plays in anywhere from 2-5 days on SoundCloud within the release. As well, the team will address other major ways of spreading newly released content through email outreach, blog outreach, and social media pushing. They will make sure your release is engaging as much audience as possible. Our entire network and affiliated networks will be involved in supporting and pushing your track.


Your track might not be accepted. If this is the case, we invite you to keep working on your craft and submitting your music in the future. 

 

In terms of PR Services, at the moment, we can only premiere a maximum of 3 songs a week and so, we are creating an easy to use solution for artists that want to be able to self-promote themselves via an extensive and professional PR plan. We are hoping this PR service will serve artists who have uploaded or plan on uploading a track onto their own SoundCloud page.  If you would like to be one of the first people to be notified about this service, feel free to drop a note to PRservices@peninsulamgmt.com. We hope to launch this very very soon ;)  

 

We hope this email has found you well and that you will submit your song to us. We are excited to hear some great music and even more excited to spread this music to the people who appreciate it. 

 

All best,

Artists Unlimited Team
